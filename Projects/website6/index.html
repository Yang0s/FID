Commission Information: GENERAL RULES

  • Commissions are not for commercial use, do not redistribute for profit.
  • Do not remove signature.
  • Editing pictures for personal use (icon,banners,etc.) is appropiate if you have purchased the commission.
  • I will not draw NSFW, Anthro, or Gore
  • I have the right to refuse a commission.

Commissions Take Time: Waitlist

  • Due to College/Commission/Personal Life You could be waitlisted for weeks at a time.
  • Payment must be the paid in full after I have accepted your commission.
  • If you wish to cancel your waitlist, please notify me.
  • You can be updated of the current progress of your commission.
